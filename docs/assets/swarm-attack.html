Throw a task in.

Each piranha holds its lane, tail sweeping, mouth shut. Mid-tank it coils, gapes, and slams — the whole body lunging into the bite, shards flying, a cyan shockwave ringing out. Drag the slider to release more.

5 piranhas

Flat lanes, no squats. Nothing rises, falls, pitches or bulges. The tail fans on its own fast clock; the body only ever drives forward and back along its line. Size, speed and bite timing are randomised per piranha, so the school never marches in step.
